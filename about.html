About AquaGuard

The sensing module hosts water sensors to test their quality to aid in data-driven solutions. It can collect data on a variety of water related pollutants including nitrates, phosphate, pH, turbidity etc. The data collected will be used to improve our understanding of the water source, pollution source , remediation materials and to help cities make informed decisions about planning and development.

About Us

Welcome To AquaGuard Website

AQUAGUARD is an open-source inspired, 3D printed, solar-powered, modular sensing device that can be easily developed to test and treat water contamination while providing real-time data on contaminants and automatically provides remediation of said contaminants. It is designed to be low-cost and easy to build, making it accessible to communities around the world. The AQUAGUARD is made of 3 modules.

The device will be connected to the device via WiFi. The application have an easy to use user interface that isn’t too complex or troublesome to use. In rural areas lacking clean water, our device offers safe access by partnering with community leaders, water manufacturers and local authorities. Addressing critical needs, we promote health, well being and sustainability for the rural Liberians.
