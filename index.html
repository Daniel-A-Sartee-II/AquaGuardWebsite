AquaGuard

AQUAGUARD is a 3D printed device equipped with various sensors.The sensing module hosts water sensors to test their quality to aid in data-driven solutions. It can test the following specific water related pollutants including pH ( Acidity or alkalinity of the water ), Total Dissolved Solids, Dissolve oxygen, Electrical Conductivity,turbidity, nitrates, and phosphate.
